III — The Story

The Story Synopsis

Every empire has a story it tells about itself.

This is the one it didn't.

While the empire celebrates its hero, a village burns quietly in the margins. This film follows Kiran and Mira — two ordinary people who once admired Veer Arka, until the day he arrived at their door.

It's about the gap between myth and truth. Between those who write history — and those erased by it.
